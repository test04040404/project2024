Hello, dear reader. And welcome to this website!

On this website you will see a few writers and their biggest masterpieces.

1)Edgar Allan Poe:

Edgar Allan Poe (1809-1849) was an American writer, one of his well-known books is 'The black cat'.
This book was published in 1845.
He died due to diseases.

2)Fyodor Dostoevsky:

Fyodor Dostoevsky (1821-1881) was a Russian writer, one of his best books called 'The Crime and Punishment'.
This book was published in 1866.
He died from a lung disorder.

3)Dazai Osamu:

Dazai Osamu (1909-1948) was a Japanese writer, he was well-known for his book: 'No longer human'.
This book was published in 1948.
He died in a double suicide, drowning himself in a rain-swollen Tamagawa Canal with a woman he loved named 'Tomie Yamazaki'.

4)Naguib Mahfouz:

Naguib Mahfouz (1911-2006) was an Egyptian writer, one of his great books of all time is 'Awllad Haretna'.
This book was published in 1959.
He died from a bleeding ulcer.
